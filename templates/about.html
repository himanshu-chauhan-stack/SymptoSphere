About SymptoSphere

SymptoSphere is a full-stack educational medical intelligence platform that predicts likely diseases from symptoms using multiple machine learning models.

Project Overview

The system compares several models, selects the highest performer, and presents top predictions with confidence scores, treatment context, and specialist recommendations.
